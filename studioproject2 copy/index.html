On beginnings

"White is the beginning of the color, and black is the end of the color."

In life, the number of beginnings
is exactly equal to the number of endings:
no one has yet to begin a life who will not end it.
In poetry, the number of beginnings so far exceeds
the number of endingsthat we cannot even conceive of it.
