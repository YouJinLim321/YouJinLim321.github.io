How to take best photoshoot of your friend

(Ingredients : smartphone, tripod, you, your friend, instagram app)

1.Text your friend to go to for a trip with you (Click this text for next step)

2.If your friend say okay, suggest her to wear the pretty clothes that suites well with the background

3.pack your smartphone, tripod, and look at the instagram feed to see an example of beautiful photos that people have taken on the location.

4.Go to the location around 2-3 in the afternoon and ask your friend to look at the sky and calm her down

5.When your friend is looking at the sky, take a photoshoot with the moderate light and try to place your friend based on the “rule of thirds“ in photography.

6.When you and your friend is ready, place the tripod and take as much photos as possible, ask your friend to pose in various ways.

7.Send the photos to your friend and ask him or her to select one photograph that satisfies your friend.
